EGirlEats

Just a girl that likes to eat.

Libby Cundiff is an Evansville local that decided to start an EGirlEats instagram page to showcase local cuisine and food during her travels.

She started an instagram in 2018 with the intent to highlight local restaurants in the Evansville, Indiana area.

Her love for food and cooking started in college when she would watch food network cooking shows.

She believes in supporting local restaurants in the community.
