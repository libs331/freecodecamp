EGirlEats

Just a girl that likes to eat.


Libby Cundiff
  • Libby Cundiff is an Evansville, Indiana local that decided to start an EGirlEats instagram page to showcase local cuisine and food during her travels as well as meals she prepared at home.
  • She started EGirlEats in 2018 with the intent to highlight local restaurants in the Evansville, Indiana area.
  • Her love for food and cooking started in college when she would watch food network cooking shows.
  • From 2005-2013, she lived in Minneapolis, MN where there were many incredible local restaurants that turned her into a foodie.
  • In 2013 she moved back to Evansville and wanted to be involved in the community so that it was more culturally rich.
  • She believes in supporting local restaurants so that Evansville can be known for having some of the best restaurants in the US.
